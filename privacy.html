Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026.

Sweep is a local-only macOS application. We designed it so that your files and the contents of your Mac never leave your machine.

What the app does with your data

Sweep scans your Mac to find files you may want to remove and moves selected items to the Trash. All of this happens entirely on your device. The cleaning engine has no network access — it does not upload, transmit, log, or share the names, contents, or locations of any files on your Mac.

The only network use: license verification

The single time Sweep contacts a server is to activate and periodically verify your license. This is handled by our payment provider, Lemon Squeezy. The only information involved is your license key and basic activation metadata (such as a device identifier so we can count active installs). No information about your files is ever sent.

Payments

Purchases are processed by Lemon Squeezy, our Merchant of Record. When you buy Sweep, your payment and billing details are collected and stored by Lemon Squeezy under their own privacy policy. We never see or store your full payment details. See Lemon Squeezy's privacy policy.

Analytics & tracking

The Sweep app contains no analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking. This website is hosted on GitHub Pages and does not set tracking cookies.
